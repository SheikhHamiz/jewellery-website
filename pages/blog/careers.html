Want to start your career with Crystals and Carats

Welcome to Crystal and Carats, where jewelry tells stories, and craftsmanship is an art. If you're passionate about creating meaningful pieces in a collaborative and creative environment, join us!

Why Crystals and Carats?

1. Artisanal Excellence:

Be part of our legacy of handcrafted excellence, where each piece is a unique masterpiece.

2. Creative Innovation:

Express your creativity in a supportive environment that values originality and fresh perspectives.

3. Commitment to Quality:

Join a team dedicated to crafting high-quality jewelry that exceeds expectations.

Current Openings:

1. Jewelry Artisan:

Bring designs to life with precision and care, showcasing your love for detail.

2. Creative Designer:

Contribute to our collection with your innovative and artistic vision.

3. Quality Assurance Specialist:

Ensure every piece meets our uncompromising standards for excellence.

4. Customer Care Advocate:

Share your passion for jewelry, providing exceptional service to help customers find their perfect piece.

How To Apply:

Ready to craft moments that last a lifetime? Send your resume and a cover letter to careers@crystalandcarats.com.
